Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information on College Study Hub.

1. Information We Collect

  • We collect your email and basic details during sign-up via Clerk for authentication purposes.
  • We store uploaded notes and related metadata for internal use only.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Your data is used only to manage your account and uploaded content.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third-party advertisers.
  • Uploaded notes are stored securely and are not shared publicly outside the platform.

3. Note Protection Policy

  • Uploaded notes are for academic sharing only within the platform.
  • Redistribution, resale, or commercial use of any note is strictly prohibited.
  • Users found violating this policy will face suspension and may be reported to their institution.

4. Data Deletion

  • You may request account or content deletion by emailing us.

5. Security

  • We use Clerk for secure user authentication and data protection.
  • Access to admin dashboard and notes approval is limited to verified accounts.
